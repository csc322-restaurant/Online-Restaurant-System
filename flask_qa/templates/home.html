Welcome to HealthySushi, an Online Sushi Restaurant System that easily allows different users to interact

with a variety of sushi restaurants.

Managers may apply to have their restaurants added. After application, they may then add their employees.

Chefs may add their own food items to their restaurant's menus.

Sales people may keep track of the ingredient orders of different restaurants.

Delivery people may keep track of customer orders.

Customers may order food items from their desired restaurants and receive benefits based on their status.

A rating system is also implemented to aid users in their decisions.

We hope that you will enjoy using HealthySushi.
